Turkey is not just for Christmas, year round it’s a firm favourite as its extra lean and very versatile.

Although you may not traditionally associate the two, it works really well in Asian dishes.

Mediterranean Mixture

  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2.5 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 Tbsp dried rosemary
  • 2 Tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 Tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 Tbsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 Tsp sea salt
  • 2 Tsp cinnamon

Directions

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl. For every 1 lb. of boneless meat or 2 lb if the meat is on the bone, add 2 tablespoons of the Mediterranean mixture.

This makes 1/3 cup.

Let it store for 24 hours—or at least 10 minutes for quicker serving.
